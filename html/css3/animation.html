animation

animation 其实是增强的过渡,从一种CSS样式过渡到另一种样子的过程中,具有了更多选择,更多控制同,更多灵活性

animation 属性介绍

  • -webkit-animation-delay 是动画开始前的延迟时间
  • -webkit-animation-direction 动画循环播放的时候是否反向播放
  • -webkit-animation-duration 动画持续时间
  • -webkit-animation-iteration-count 动画播放次数
  • -webkit-animation-name 指定动画名称
  • -webkit-animation-play-state 允许动画暂停和重新播放
  • -webkit-animation-timing-function 动画模式

animation简写

animation: animation-name animation-duration animation-timing-function animation-delay animation-iteration-count

示例;

China established a strategic partnership with the Czech Republic on Tuesday during the visit by President Xi Jinping to the European nation. It is the first state visit by a Chinese president since diplomatic ties were established with the former Czechoslovakia 67 years ago. Xi and his Czech counterpart Milos Zeman signed a joint statement on the partnership after official talks. They also witnessed the signing of nine documents, covering fields such as electronic business and investment.
